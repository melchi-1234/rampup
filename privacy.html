RampUp — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Short version: RampUp collects nothing. Your data never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. RampUp does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There is no account, no sign-up, and no login.

Data stored on your device

If you turn on "Remember my progress" in Settings, RampUp saves your practice statistics, streak, daily goal, review pile, and bookmarks locally on your device (in the app's local storage). This information stays on your device, is never sent anywhere, and is used only to show you your own progress. You can erase it at any time from the app's Settings ("Reset everything").

Network & third parties

RampUp works fully offline. It makes no network requests and includes no analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers of any kind.

Children

RampUp does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
