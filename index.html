The loop

Studying that hooks like your favorite app

Every piece of the game loop is here, wrapped around real MCAT content that actually teaches.

Climb the learning path

Your home is a path that winds through every MCAT section. Clear a node's bite-sized session to unlock the next, ace it for gold, and watch your progress build like levels in a game.

XP, levels & combos

Earn XP for every correct answer, chain combos for bonus points, and climb from Pre-Pre-Med to Future Doctor.

Streaks with freezes

Build a daily habit. Miss a day and a streak freeze saves you, so one busy afternoon never erases your momentum.

Spaced repetition

Miss a question and it comes back right before you would forget it, so the things you get wrong become the things you remember.

Explain from scratch

Stuck with no idea where to start? Tap once for a plain-language breakdown that builds the concept up from zero.

Private by design

No account, no sign-up, no tracking. Everything you do stays on your device, and once the page loads it keeps working with no connection at all. It is your study buddy, not a data funnel.

Why RampUp
